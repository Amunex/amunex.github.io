TEDxIE Madri

Brand Leadership & Event Strategy

While pursuing my master’s at IE University, I was selected to be the Brand Leader for TEDxIE Madrid. I led a six-person creative team responsible for the entire brand presence of the event, from visual identity and social media to live event production.

The year’s theme was “Mirror, Mirror”, a vague prompt that required creative interpretation. I spearheaded the conceptual breakdown and established a clear narrative foundation, connecting the theme to self-reflection, digital duality, and identity. This guided the tone for all content moving forward.

I oversaw the full production pipeline for the event’s visual identity — from social content to venue design. I personally created animation templates in After Effects, which allowed the team to replicate dynamic visual posts with ease. I ran hands-on training sessions to help team members — many of whom had little motion design experience — learn how to produce quality social content on tight timelines. I also implemented a content calendar for rollout, mapped all brand touchpoints across the attendee journey, and ensured that every asset, from speaker graphics to stage visuals, aligned with our narrative and design system.

Beyond visuals, I supported core event logistics. I coordinated with guest speakers, helped structure the day’s schedule, and brought in a live music performance by directly connecting local musicians — an element that helped elevate the experience beyond the traditional TED format. I worked across teams, stepped into operational gaps, and helped problem-solve live challenges as they arose, from tech setup to speaker flow. My creative leadership extended far past visuals — it shaped the rhythm and tone of the event as a whole.

Ultimately, the event sold out, social engagement soared, and our content was shared widely across IE’s internal channels. TEDxIE Madrid became one of the most talked-about editions in recent years — thanks to a narrative that resonated, a brand system that held it together, and a team that knew exactly what they were building.

Contributions

  • Led a 6-person creative team as Brand Leader
  • Interpreted and defined the core narrative behind “Mirror, Mirror”
  • Developed visual identity, tone, and content plan
  • Created animated social assets using AE + trained team members to replicate workflow
  • Coordinated live band, helped secure speakers, and supported logistics for a 200+ person event

Results

  • Event reached full attendance with all tickets sold out
  • Substantial social media growth through branded content
  • Cross-functional team learned new tools and shipped cohesive visuals
  • Delivered one of the most memorable editions of TEDxIE Madrid to date
  • Achieved a 65% increase in social media engagement during the 3-week pre-event campaign
  • Grew TEDxIE Madrid’s Instagram followers by +340% (from ~180 to 800+) through branded reels and daily content
  • Reached 18,000+ unique accounts organically via motion-designed story campaigns
  • 100% of creative content was delivered on time by a fully non-designer team trained on basic animation workflow
