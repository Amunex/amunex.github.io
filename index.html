About me

Motion Designer

Dual American & Chilean Citizen | Motion Designer

Hey there! I'm Matías Amunátegui, a Motion Designer with a zest for storytelling through various media. Originally from Chile, I now call the United States home. My diverse cultural background and extensive travels give me a unique perspective in design and communication.

I thrive in a fast-paced environment, excelling at meeting tight deadlines while consistently generating fresh ideas and concepts. Although some of my work is under NDA, you can explore my motion graphics and motion comics projects on my website and YouTube channel for a taste of my creativity.

Let's dive in and get inspired!
