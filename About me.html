About Matias

Born and raised in Chile with American roots, I merge the best of both worlds—analytical thinking and creative instinct. Bilingual, culturally fluent, and built for cross-disciplinary storytelling.

Design by Instinct.
Strategy by Practice.

Most marketers don’t know the difference between “making things pop” and making things work. Most designers think branding stops at a style guide. I know better, because I’ve sat on both sides.
To sharpen it, I earned a degree in Digital Design. Then, I went deeper: a Master’s in Digital Marketing & Analytics. Most designers think they get marketing. Most marketers think they get design. I’m the rare one who actually does both.
I understand user behavior, performance funnels, and the emotional arcs that make people click, sign up, and remember you.

The Bridge'd Gap 

With digital marketing and digital design at my fingertips, I don’t just bridge the gap—I work from the middle. Most designers don’t understand data. Most marketers forget storytelling. I’ve studied and practiced both. That means I can craft visuals that convert, campaigns that communicate, and strategies that move with purpose. This isn’t surface-level fluency—it’s native thinking on both sides of the brain. When brands need both performance and presence, that’s where I fit in.

A Little More Human

I’m a drummer. I’ve played in bands, in bars, on stages, and in studios. Music has taught me rhythm, tempo, restraint, and collaboration. It’s no surprise I love editing video, building soundscapes, and shaping moments that land just right.
I run Dungeons & Dragons campaigns with friends. I read about space for fun. I build worlds in my head and bring them to life through visuals. I don’t just design—I worldbuild. Strategy and storytelling are the same to me: it’s all about making someone feel something. Curiosity is my compass. Execution is my standard.

Creative Direction Meets Performance Strategy

I specialize in building creative that performs: data-backed, narrative-rich, strategically sound campaigns. From motion graphics to multi-platform funnels, I lead with clarity of vision and a deep understanding of how audiences think, feel, and behave. I don’t just make things look good—I make them work.
From corporate clients to musicians, nonprofits, politicians, and startups—I’ve done it all. I’ve led motion comic teams, art directed brand launches, built social ad campaigns from scratch, and designed entire content ecosystems. Every deliverable? On brief, on message, and on purpose.
